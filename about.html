ABOUT ME

I am Pavel Satrov, ICT Specialist from Helsinki, Finland.

I have worked as an ICT specialist in an accredited inspection company for the past seven years. During this time, I have been involved in various IT-related tasks.

Throughout my tenure, I have been involved in a wide range of IT-related tasks, allowing me to develop a diverse skill set. As a second-level technical support for the outsourced service desk, I have collaborated with different stakeholders and worked closely with vendors to enhance our internal processes. This collaborative effort has significantly improved the service desk's ability to effectively serve our company's end-users. Additionally, I have successfully coordinated the day-to-day tasks of the ICT team, including equipment procurement, maintenance, leasing exchanges, and technical support.

One of my key responsibilities has been maintaining the equipment inventory, managing user accounts, and configuring network devices. These tasks have honed my technical expertise and attention to detail, ensuring the smooth operation of our company's IT infrastructure. Furthermore, I have been the primary user of our organization's proprietary applications, allowing me to gain in-depth knowledge of their functionality and troubleshoot issues efficiently.

In my role, I have embraced independence and initiative, continuously seeking opportunities for professional growth and process improvement. I have taken the initiative to generate numerous development ideas to enhance our company's ICT processes. Working closely with my team, we have refined these ideas into functional processes that have significantly benefited both the business and the service desk.

I am confident that my technical proficiency, collaborative mindset, and proactive approach make me a valuable asset to any team. I am enthusiastic about leveraging my skills and experience to contribute to your organization and drive its IT initiatives forward.
